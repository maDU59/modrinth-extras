# Privacy Policy

Modrinth Extras shares anonymous statistics to help improve the extension. This telemetry can be disabled at any time in the extension settings.

## What is collected

- When the extension starts, is installed, or is updated (extension version and platform)
- Which features of the extension are used

## What is not collected

- No personal information
- No Modrinth account data or activity
- No browsing history or page content

## Where data is sent

Data is sent to a [PostHog](https://posthog.com) instance operated by the extension author. It is not shared with third parties.
